Hello! I am Raquel Bonilla :) I am a web developer and graphic designer. I was born in Colombia. Since I was a kid, I’ve always love everything related with arts, designing and logical thinking.. Currently I work as a freelancer

I have always been passionate about art and logic. I started my journey as a young artist, learning drawing and animation. I later developed my skills in graphic design, learning programs like Animate CC, Illustrator, and Photoshop. I also learned English, and developed a high level of conversation, listening, and speaking.

My passion for technology led me to study Systems Engineering. I am currently in my fifth semester program at the National Open and Distance University. In addition to my studies, I completed an Oracle Next Education scholarship where I specialized as a Front-End Developer with an emphasis on React.js. I have gained a lot of experience through freelance work and autonomous learning. I am always eager to learn new skills and I am confident that I can make a significant contribution to any problem or challenge!

In parallel, I developed my skills in web development and design. I started with C#, and later focused on the front-end, with CSS, HTML and learning programming languages like JavaScript and TypeScript. I also worked with web page design using CMS platforms like WordPress, Shopify, Blogger, Joomla, Drupal and among others.
